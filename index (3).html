🐱 How to Buy $BOBIN

Follow these simple steps to purchase $BOBIN on the Ethereum blockchain:

  • 📝 Step 1: Create a Wallet
    Download MetaMask or your wallet of choice from the App Store or Google Play Store for free. If you are using a desktop, you can download the Google Chrome extension by visiting metamask.io.
  • 💎 Step 2: Get Some ETH
    You need to have ETH in your wallet in order to switch to $BOBIN. If you don’t have any ETH, you can buy directly through MetaMask, transfer from another wallet, or buy ETH on another exchange and send it to your wallet.
  • 🦄 Step 3: Go to Uniswap
    Visit app.uniswap.org in Google Chrome, or use the browser inside your MetaMask app. Connect your wallet to Uniswap. Paste the $BOBIN token address into Uniswap, select $BOBIN, and confirm the transaction. When MetaMask prompts you for a wallet signature, make sure to sign the transaction.
  • 🔄 Step 4: Swap ETH for $BOBIN
    Now, swap ETH for $BOBIN. You don’t need to worry about using a specific slippage setting, although during market volatility, you may need to adjust slippage for a successful transaction.
